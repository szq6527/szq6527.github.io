I am **Zhengqi Sun (孙政奇)**, an undergraduate (class of 2023) at the [Department of Information Management](https://www.im.pku.edu.cn/), [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/), pursuing a double degree in **General Artificial Intelligence** at [Yuanpei College](https://yuanpei.pku.edu.cn/). I currently work on world models in the **Embodied and Generative Intelligence Lab** led by [Prof. Hao Tang](https://ha0tang.github.io/) (School of Computer Science).

My primary research interests are **world models** and **embodied intelligence** — building agents that imagine the consequences of their actions and act safely in the physical world. I also work on **large language models (LLMs)** and **synthetic data**. Concretely, my work spans:

- **World models & embodied intelligence** — coupling reasoning with action-conditioned world models for safe, closed-loop decision making in embodied settings such as autonomous driving. See our ITSC 2026 oral paper, *Reason–Imagine–Act*.
- **LLMs & synthetic data** — an information-theoretic account of *when synthetic data helps*: only when the generation–training loop is **information-open**, otherwise the data processing inequality predicts model collapse (ICML 2026).
- **Graph learning for finance** — heterogeneous temporal graph neural networks for credit-risk contagion prediction (ITQM 2026).

I am broadly interested in trustworthy and efficient machine learning, world models, and reinforcement learning from verifiable signals. Please find my [publications](/publications/), [projects](/projects/), and [CV](/cv/) here, and feel free to reach out by email.
